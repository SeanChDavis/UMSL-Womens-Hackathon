Fonts & Colors

In the past, a common font choice was Montserrat, which is a Google font. It is available for free and can be used in any design software. The font is available in multiple weights, making it versatile for various design needs. You may also download and install Montserrat on your local machine.

Montserrat 900  |  Montserrat 800  |  Montserrat 700  |  Montserrat 600  |  Montserrat 400

Colors are also important to consider when designing. You are not bound to these colors, but they are a good starting point. The colors below are the ones used in the past. The hex codes are provided for easy reference. Always consider color contrast when designing. The colors should be accessible to all users, including those with visual impairments.

Black

#000000

2025 Gradient

#F8C03A, #F63535, #912EA6

2025 Gradient (alt)

#8680DD, #5148D3, #0B0559, #030032

Gray

#f9f9f9

Note: Both "2025 Gradient" swatches are linear, angled at 135 degrees. These were used by the 2025 class. The 2024 team used a different gradient. Consider creating a new gradient to be unique while using the same foundation. Coolors.co Gradient Maker is a great tool for creating gradients.
